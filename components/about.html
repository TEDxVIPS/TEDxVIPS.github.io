About TEDx

TEDx is a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. At a TEDx event, TED Talks videos and live speakers combine to spark deep discussion and connection. These events are branded TEDx, where x = independently organized TED event, operating under a free license granted by TED.

"Our goal is to provide a platform for thinkers, visionaries, and learners, fostering a community dedicated to the power of ideas."
