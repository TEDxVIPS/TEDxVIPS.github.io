About TEDx

TEDx is a grassroots initiative, created in the spirit of TED’s overall mission to research and discover “ideas worth spreading.” TEDx events are independently organized under a free license granted by TED, bringing people together to share a TED-like experience. These local, self-organized events feature a combination of live presenters and TED Talk videos, designed to spark deep discussion, connection, and inspiration within local communities. The TEDx program aims to foster learning, inspire innovation, and provoke conversations that matter on a local scale, thereby extending the global impact of TED's vision. While TED provides general guidance for the TEDx program, individual TEDx events, including ours, are self-organized and operate independently, adhering to a framework that ensures a consistent, high-quality experience for attendees worldwide.
